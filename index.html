Welcome to Zootopia

From tiny toads to big cats, there are more than 6,000 species at the Rosemoor Zoo.
Plan a visit to see your favorite member of the animal kingdom and meet some new ones along the way!

Fantastic Wings

You can see birds in the nature surrounded by trees. Have a special experience in which freely flying birds fly to me.

Lion Valley

Lion Valley recreated the habitat of Lions with cool waterfalls, ponds, and birch forests.

Bird Paradise

Birds of a feather flock paradise. You can see many beautiful birds, including the dancing elegant flamingo, and so on.

Penguin Paradise

Zakas Penguin from South Africa. You can see the penguins with cute gait. With the blue sky at the largest exhibition hall.
